How do you wear a mask properly?

Steps:

1. Hold the mask by the edges and straps only.
2. Make sure the blue side is facing outside and the white side is facing inwards.
3. Place it over your mouth and nose with the stiff edge over your nose.
4. Loop the strap behind your ear.
5. Pinch the stiff edge and pull the bottom under your chin.
