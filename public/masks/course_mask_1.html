What types of masks are there?

There are three types of masks that work well to minimise the spread of coronavirus. These are:

  • Surgical masks - usually blue ones like the ones doctors use. These are usually single use.
  • p2 or n95 masks - they can be single use or reusable.
  • Cloth masks - usually washable and reusable
