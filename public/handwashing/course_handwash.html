Handwashing Technique

COVID-19 germs from things you touch can stay on your hands for many hours. That's why you need to wash your hands regularly. If you touch your face with germs on your hands you could get sick too! If you cannot wash your hands with soap and water, you can also use hand sanitiser. Follow the below technique to learn how to best clean your hands.

Make sure you do this for at least 20 seconds! This is the amount of time it takes to sing:
- Happy Birthday once.
- The line “We will, we will, rock you” 3 times.
- Twinkle, Twinkle Little Star twice.
